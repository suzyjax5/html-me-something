Professional Background

My professional background takes on three aspects:

  1. Technology
  2. Education
  3. Finance

Technology is a natural part of almost every career in this age. And I have always sought out positions that leaned toward technology. Many years ago, I worked as a programmer/analyst for a large financial services firm. I left that position to take on a Operations Manager position which lead to becoming the head of the IT Projects Office. Currently, I work as the Core Data Manager for a local school district. I find this job enjoyable because it allows me to work in both technology and education. It also taps into my love for data.

The majority of my professional experience in education has been in libraries. I have worked as both a school librarian and a librarian for a state facility. Both allowed me to teach technology, especially in its use in research and finding data. In addition to being a certified Library Media Specialist, I am also currently certified to teach Social Scienc, Mathematics, and Technology and Engineering.

My initial professional experience was in the financial services industry. I have worked for several firms headquarted in St. Louis. I was a licensed stock broker and principal. The latter allowed me to supervise other stock brokers as well as serve in a compliance role for the firms where I worked. I liked this experience because it lead to my work in technology where I was able to use my industry knowledge in this new (for me) area.

Educational Background
